About Me

A Quick Introduction

Hi! I'm Oliver, an -year-old student at Caltech. I like coding, coffee, and photography (especially when I'm taking pictures of space). Check out my maker projects (bottom panel), or my GitHub and LinkedIn profiles, if you're feeling especially serious.

Accomplishments?

I was an intern at the W.M. Keck Observatory on Mauna Kea, Hawaii, from 2016 to 2018. I wrote planning programs for the support astronomy team to prepare for observing, created a 3D map of the moon Io, and built an interface to save and retrieve telescope configurations at the last minute.
